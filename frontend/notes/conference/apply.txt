How to Apply
Take Financial Training
 👉 https://webcourses.ucf.edu/enroll/4FCC68
Apply on KnightConnect
Allocation (for smaller trips): at least 4 weeks before travel
https://knightconnect.campuslabs.com/engage/submitter/form/start/636439
Bill (for larger or RSO trips): at least 12 weeks before travel
https://knightconnect.campuslabs.com/engage/submitter/form/start/636440
Attend a CRT Meeting (you’ll get emailed with details)
 Be ready to talk briefly about your trip and show updated quotes.
After Approval:
Wait for the Travel Procurement Coordinator (TPC) to contact you.
Don’t buy anything until you meet with them — no reimbursements before that!
After the Trip:
 Submit receipts and proof of travel within 30 days.

⚠️ Quick Tips
Don’t skip CRT meetings — missing 2 without notice means automatic denial
Use 80–95% of your awarded funds, or your RSO may face penalties
Ask questions early: email sga_crt@ucf.edu
