How to Apply
1. Complete Financial Training
    https://webcourses.ucf.edu/enroll/4FCC68
2. Apply on KnightConnect
Allocation (for smaller trips): at least 4 weeks before travel
https://knightconnect.campuslabs.com/engage/submitter/form/start/636439
Bill (for larger or RSO trips): at least 12 weeks before travel
https://knightconnect.campuslabs.com/engage/submitter/form/start/636440
3. Attend Your CRT Meeting 
 You’ll receive details by email. Be ready to speak briefly about your trip and show quotes.
4. After Approval
Wait for your Travel Procurement Coordinator (TPC) to contact you.
Don’t make purchases before meeting them – no reimbursements!
5. After the Trip
 Submit receipts and proof of travel within 30 days.

Quick Tips
Missing 2 meetings without notice = automatic denial
Use 80–95% of your funds, or your RSO may be penalized
Questions? email sga_crt@ucf.edu
