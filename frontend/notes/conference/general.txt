What CRT Can Pay For
CRT helps students or RSOs (Registered Student Organizations) cover costs for academic or professional trips, namely Lodging (Hotel near the event), Registration (Conference or competition fees), and Travel (Main way to get there; flight, bus, etc.)

The Yearly Maximums for RSOs are $4,500 per RSO per fiscal year and $1,000 per for individuals per fiscal year. Fiscal years run from July 1 to June 30 of the succeeding year.
